An in-depth discussion of this visualization and some conclusions

Religion & Rights

For this visualization, I wanted to explore a common claim (amoung religious liberty-focused academians) that religious freedom is an indicator of all other rights, such that a decline in religious freedom is a foreshadowing of decline in other fundamental rights. The following bubble chart explores this claim, in a style very remniscent of Hans Rosling's work. Below, feel free to interact with the visualization and watch how religious freedom relates to other fundamental rights. The bubbles each represent countries, sized according to their population. In the upper left, you can select which fundamental right to be measured on the y-axis. On the right, select any year between 1975 and 2019. Hover over an individual bubble to see details about that country.
